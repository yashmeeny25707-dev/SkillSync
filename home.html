Workflow

How it should work

Resume Analyzer ka flow simple rakha gaya hai: pehle SkillSync ke through resume generate hota hai, phir analyzer usi generated data ko check karke improvement suggestions deta hai.

01

Create resume

User apna resume SkillSync Resume Generator me banata hai.

02

Fetch data

Analyzer generated resume ke sections aur content ko fetch karta hai.

03

Analyze quality

ATS structure, missing keywords, readability, aur completeness check hoti hai.

04

Show improvements

User ko clear bataya jata hai kya sahi hai aur kya improve karna chahiye.
